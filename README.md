`trusted-users`' creed:

> This is my Nix config. There are many like it, but this one is mine.
>
> My config is my best friend. It is my life. I must master it as I must master
> my life.
>
> Without my Yubikey, my config is useless. Without my config, my Yubikey is
> useless. I must activate my profile true. I must activate it more immutably
> than my programs who are trying to create mutable state. I must create
> immutable files before they create mutable ones. I will ...
>
> My config and I know that what counts in programming is not the lines of code
> we write, the noise of our keyboards, nor the logs our programs emit. We know
> that it is the configs that count. We will configure ...
>
> My config is human, even as I [am human], because it is my life. Thus, I will
> learn it as a sibling. I will learn its weaknesses, its strength, its modules,
> its options, its metadata and its activation command. I will keep my config
> clean and ready, even as I am clean and ready. We will become part of each
> other. We will ...
> 
> Before `nix-daemon`, I swear this creed. My config and I are the defenders of
> my computer. We are the masters of our state. We are the saviors of my life.
>
> So be it, until victory is Nix's and there is no state, but purity!
